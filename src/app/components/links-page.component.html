Here you can find urls to interesting online repositories on comparative historical linguistics (not restricted to IE).

CLLD

visit clld.org
Cross-linguistic linked data, comprising multiple specialized databases.
Project of Forkel, R. & Haspelmath, M.

Glottolog

visit glottolog.org
Master bibliography of paper and digital sources & language data.
Part of the CLLD project by Hammarstrom, H. & Forkel, R. & Haspelmath, M.

EIEOL Lessons & Lexicon

visit utexas.edu
Digitized version of the Pokorny Dictionary of 1959 (Indogermanisches etymologisches Worterbuch), the Pokorny Master Collection.
Also Early Indo-European Online lessons, from the University of Texas at Austin Linguistics Research Center.
Project of Slocum, J.and Lehman, W. P.
